Which walky talkies can be bought without any licensing needs from Indian Government? Which walky talkies can be bought without any licensing needs from Indian Government?

The devices that are in accordance to the technical specifications of Personal Mobile Radio 446 MHz mentioned in Table No.V of PART II-Section 3-Sub-section (i) Gazette notification issued by The Government of India on October 18, 2018 can be bought in the market without taking any license from the Indian Government. The models we offer under the License-Free category are Vertel Walky-Talky LF, Smart Talky & Team-Talky.
