Analog Handheld (VL-747)

Vertel Hand Held Portable Radios offer longer range and are known for reliability, stable performance and operation in most rugged environment. Vertel VL-747 walkie talkie is a compact and ergonomically designed handheld walkie talkie Radio available in VHF and UHF frequency bands. The Radio offers longer communication range, crisp and loud audio and longer lasting battery life. VL-747 has been tested by NABL approved laboratories to meet extreme environmental conditions for operations.
